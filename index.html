Meaning of roses' colors

  • Red roses are an expression of love, longing, and desire.
  • White roses stand for new beginnings, sympathy, humility, and spirituality.
  • Yellow roses represent joy, warmth, and welcome. Unlike other rose colors, yellow doesn’t signify love, but instead more friendship and caring.
  • Pink roses represent admiration, joy, and gratitude
  • Orange roses signify passion and energy as well as desire, pride, and fervor.
